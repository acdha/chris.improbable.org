Saturday cycling

Another great day for a ride. I'm feeling lazy so I'm turning around here in San Clemente. Current distance: 62mi

Update: Total trip time: 7 hours, 39 minutes. My first double metric-century, which is good but I was certainly feeling a lot more sluggish than last month. Oddly enough, it wasn't the extra distance - I was a little slower throughout the entire day, with an average of 16mph.
