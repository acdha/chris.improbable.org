Why I live in San Diego

A perfect day up to Capistrano and it looks great for the ride home...

Updated: That turned out to be a very accurate prediction: great conditions and smooth cycling, for a total of 101.8 miles at an average of 17.2MPH, which is a healthy 8MPH improvement over the first time I did that ride two years ago. The best part is that the 38MPH peak was set in the last four miles I did through UCSD to push the total over 100 miles.
