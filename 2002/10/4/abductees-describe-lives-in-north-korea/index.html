Abductees describe lives in North Korea

Finally, some details about a surprisingly ignored story: the people abducted by the North Korean government courtesy of the Daily Yomiuri:

Soga was taken to North Korea in August 1978 by a Japanese who was collaborating with Pyongyang's abduction operations, according to North Korean officials.

The officials quoted her as saying: "I was told I was brought here to study Korean. Although I longed to return to Japan, I didn't have the power to do that."
