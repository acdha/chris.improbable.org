Mobile IP and 3G / 802.11 roaming!

Lucent demonstrated a wireless device seamlessly moving from a 3G cellular network to a WiFi network. This is the holy grail of wireless but, even more importantly, it's a real world use of Mobile IP.

Mobile IP is high on the list of inexplicably underused technologies. Basically it allows devices to roam across different networks and keep the same IP address so their traffic won't be interrupted. A PDA or laptop with the technology Lucent demonstrated would be able to pick the fastest available network at any point in time without affecting your connections. Start a download which is too slow over 3G? Just move inside and it will transparently switch up to the faster WiFi network. The other benefit is consistency - your system will be mylaptop.example.com no matter which network you happen to be on at the time.
