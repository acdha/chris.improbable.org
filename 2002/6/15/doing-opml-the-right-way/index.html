Doing OPML the right way

Does anyone maintain a guide to being a good neighbour when using OPML? I spent twenty minutes creating OPML and RSS feeds. Most of the OPML files I looked at used a number of custom attributes in addition to the ones required by OPML 1.0 and there were the tantalizing references to the unpublished OPML 1.1, too.

It strikes me that it would be useful to have a list implementors could check: "adding this will make your OPML more useful to people using these applications". Does anyone maintain such a list?
