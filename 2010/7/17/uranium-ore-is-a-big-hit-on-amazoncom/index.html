Uranium Ore Is A Big Hit On Amazon.com

The "Customers who viewed this also viewed..." list contains some other great items, some well known, like the Tuscan Whole Milk, others I've not seen before (like the long awaited digital edition of "The 2009-2014 Outlook for Wood Toilet Seats in Greater China")

For those of you who enjoy creative amazon reviews, allow us to direct you to Uranium Ore.

The funniest review: "I purchased this product 4.47 Billion Years ago and when I opened it today, it was half empty."

Over on Reddit they've pointed out that the can wouldn't actually be empty, just full of other elements.

It's still funny, though. Enjoy.
