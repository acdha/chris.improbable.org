Drobo Backup

This is why I switched to online backup. CrashPlan just works and it saves me the home sysadmin work I used to waste too much time on.

Tim Wood / @tjw:

Why yes Drobo, I would like to you flash all your lights and tell me my data isn’t protected but not tell me why. Thanks.

Tim Wood / @tjw:

Oh. Now it says “No Drobo is detected.” Luckily everything on this device is a backup… of the other Drobo. Confidence - -

I had a similar scare with my Drobo, so I bought a couple of 2 TB drives, popped them into my Voyager Q toasters and created a software RAID 1 volume.

I’m in the process of copying my data from my Drobo to the old-fashioned-but-I-understand-it RAID.

I don’t plan on buying any more Drobos. I was excited for ZFS, but it looks like I’ll just have to wait out Btrfs and/or Apple’s new file system they’re working on.
