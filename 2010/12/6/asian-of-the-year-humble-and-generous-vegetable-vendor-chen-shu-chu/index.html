Asian of the year, humble and generous vegetable vendor, Chen Shu-chu

Chen has a daily routineâ€”waking up at 3am, she makes her way to the vegetable wholesaler and sets up her stall, which she tends till seven or eight in the evening. The first to arrive in the dark, damp market and the last to leave, other stall-owners have fondly nicknamed her 'market manager.' Chen holds the stall her father left her dearly. Yuan-Jin Vegetables is her everything. Selling at "a bundle for 30 dollars*, three bundles for 50," Chen earns only marginal profits. Yet, her frugality has allowed her to donate about NT$10 million (nearly Rs1.5 crore) [approx. US$330,000] towards various charitable causes, including helping schools, orphanages and poor children.
