Desaturated Santa Blows San Franciscans Minds


Credit: Tom Hilton/Flickr

For the second year in a row, the brilliant BrodyQat stunned SantaCon revelers and spectators with her Desaturated Santa Costume.

No, it's not Photoshopped! Says BrodyQuat on Reddit: "It's a custom grey Santa suit sewn by me, grey body paint from Kryolan (the theatrical makeup company), grey wig, and grey contacts."

Also, her bag "contained creepy altered stuffed animals to give out to people (bears with 2 heads, things sewn to bits of other things, etc)." Awesome!
