Win an Android from AndroidSPIN and Wirefly!

We’ve all been there; those seemingly endless arguements with that friend who thinks the iPhone is the perfect representation of what a smartphone should be. Don’t you just wish you tell them once and for all how lame iPhone’s really are, and have them listen to you?

We try to keep the Android/Apple rivalry civil around here, but now we’re asking you to take the gloves off, so to speak, and let your iPhone hatred fly for a good cause: you! Who wouldn’t be down for a little trash talk, if it gets you a brand new Android of your choice!

The rules are easy:

Tell the world why you think an Android™ phone can lay the smack down on the iPhone®, and you could win your pick of ANY Android-powered phone from Wirefly’s vast selection (HTC EVO 4G included!). Five (5) winners will be selected.

Entries will be judged based on wit, intelligence and creativity – so bring your ‘A’-game, and keep it a clean fight. The entry period will close June 28th, and authors of the five best entries will be notified by email soon after if they’ve delivered a written knockout blow.

Be sure to cheer on your favorites and call out the wannabes by giving each response a resounding “thumbs up” or “thumbs down.”

You know you would love to show off your brand new Android to that iPhone-loving friend. So what are you waiting for? Head here to start dropping your best iPhone put-downs!
