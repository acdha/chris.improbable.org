GW student proposes 'Christian only' swim hour

This fall, George Washington University began offering women-only sessions at the school's Foggy Bottom indoor pool. The one-hour-a-week single-sex swim is meant to accommodate female Muslim students whose religious beliefs preclude them from swimming with men.

One white, conservative Christian woman, despite being inconvenienced neither by the pool's regular swim hours nor by the women-only session, takes exception to this arrangement.

"As a female, I feel the need to speak up regarding the idiocy of this most recent example of politically correct nonsense. Namely, the audacity of creating female-only swim hours to 'accommodate' female Muslim students," Erin Mew, a GWU freshman who adds "a cynical and pensive editorial voice" to campus conservative publication the GW Patriot, wrote on Tuesday. "The fundamental issues I have with female-only swim hours are as follows," she continued. "[Y]ou are asking everyone to accommodate your cultural/religious beliefs, and subsequently asking to be treated differently from everyone else. Likewise, you are asking every male student to get out of the pool because of your 7th century mindset, which is essentially sexist."

Also, it is Muslim!:

I understand that outside of the female-only hours, men and women are free to swim with whomever they please. But I am concerned that we strive to be so politically correct that we would be willing to compromise on some basic Western principles, all while sanctioning the further Islamization of America. I am referring specifically to the idea that men and women should be able to coexist as equals. How is this served by allowing institutions to support a quarantine on swimming for men or women? What happened to the crusade fought by feminists to level the playing field; to destroy any semblance of treating women differently in Western society? And why aren’t male students outraged at this? If you posses an X and a Y chromosome, you cannot swim in the pool for an hour because the university needs prove it is tolerant of Islam.

When a Christian's entitlement to constant indoor swimming pool access conflicts with her outward tolerance of Islam, the pool always wins. Why not settle this once and for all by just denying Muslim women something?

So as a white, conservative, Christian woman, I would like to make a proposition. I would like to have a Christian-only swim hour once a week at the Lerner Health and Wellness Center. That isn’t too much to ask, is it? Maybe I do not want to swim with certain people because they make me feel uncomfortable. Shouldn’t the university demonstrate to me that it is tolerant of my lifestyle and my beliefs?

If your lifestyle includes inventing hardships in order to demand accommodations you have never before desired in the service of justifying totally made-up religious beliefs for the sole purpose of exacting revenge on Muslims, rest assured that the George Washington University already tolerates this pursuit.
