Pro Git on your iPad

Works quite nicely in Stanza on an iPhone, too

The awesome guys at Media Temple have converted the Pro Git book into ePub format that looks great on the iBook reader on the iPad.

You can download it here and just drop the ePub file onto iTunes to upload it into your iPad. For reading Pro Git on the road, this is a great format and MT did an amazing job at making it look good on that platform.
