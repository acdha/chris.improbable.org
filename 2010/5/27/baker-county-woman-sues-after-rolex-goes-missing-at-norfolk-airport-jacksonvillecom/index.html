Baker County woman sues after Rolex goes missing at Norfolk airport | jacksonville.com

Security cameras sure seem to malfunction a lot in cases where official misconduct is alleged. If it was actually so common, you'd think we'd have some sort of massive suit against the manufacturers for selling defective security equipment…

Imagine being ordered by airport security workers to put your very expensive watch on a scanning belt, over your objections. Then imagine going to retrieve it and it’s gone. A Baker County woman says that experience at Norfolk International Airport in Virginia last year cost her a $24,000 Rolex her husband saved up to buy her on her 50th birthday. Now she’s suing the U.S. Transportation Security Administration after the agency rejected her claim letter and her congressman unsuccessfully attempted to intervene.
