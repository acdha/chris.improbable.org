It’s a bird of prey…it’s a plane hijacking…no, wait, it’s a crazy cat lady!

By Thoreau

This sums up what I think about Jihad Jane:

Without wishing to undermine her twin commitments to holy war and talking to cats, the self-styled Jihad Jane might be the war on terror’s least effective bogeywoman.

If she went overseas plotting murder, clearly that cannot be tolerated.  Do something about it.  But, like I said when I blogged about the (mistaken) reports that Adam Gadahn had been arrested, don’t put her in a military brig and move her around with the tightest security available and wring your hands about how hard it is to try her.  That just feeds the super-villain fantasy.  Instead, lock her in the county jail with thieves and other criminals.  Put her in the usual unflattering orange jumpsuit, not the special sensory-deprivation headgear that Jose Padilla was marched around in.  (For that matter, get Padilla out of the sensory deprivation headgear too, and just give him the usual orange jumpsuit.)  Don’t let her think she’s anything special, because she isn’t.  She isn’t Annabelle Lecter.  She isn’t Mystique.  She’s just another criminal.  The fact that she said the word “Jihad” and talked to some angry Muslims doesn’t change the fact that she’s just another criminal, and a pathetic one at that.
