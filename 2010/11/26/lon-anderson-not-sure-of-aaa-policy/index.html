Lon Anderson not sure of AAA policy

Frequent commenter Jonathan Krall took the opportunity during his chat on the post to ask AAA Mid-Atlantic's Lon Anderson about their policy on funding Transportation Enhancements

AAA Mid-Atlantic has called for the Federal Transportation Fund to be used exclusively for roads--no transit funding at all. Do you therefore recommend that as many Americans as possible do their holiday travel by car?

To which he replied

I always enjoy it when folks tell me what AAA policy is.  We do not and have not ever called for defunding transit.  Period. And if you can make alternate modes work for you this holiday, by all means, do so.  I'm on Amtrak almost every week myself. Have a great holiday, and get there safely, by what ever means you choose. We favor transportation choices.

Which is true only because he didn't answer the question asked. He was not asked about generally about AAA wanting to defund transit, he was asked about AAA demanding that the entire Highway Trust Fund to be used fr roads, which as it turns out, they did do. This is the same tactic they used in response to rails to trails when they claimed they did not ask to defund trail, walking and biking programs. What they did say was

Why not let the Highway Trust Fund pay for our highways as intended, and let the general revenues address the other expenses?

Which is saying "we don't want to defund any of these things, we just want to take away the mechanism by which they've always been funded." RTC responds

AAA's position would eliminate Transportation Enhancements (TE) and other long-standing programs that build active transportation facilities such as trails. Starting over with new programs is especially impractical in the face of a possible budget freeze. Even if budgets were not so tight, to divorce active transportation funding from the transportation finance system would deny state and local officials needed flexibility to develop a healthy mix of transportation choices.

Remember, there's always Better World Club.

BTW, AAA Mid-Atlantic's office is right next to a trail that was paid for TE money. A trail they built a sidewalk to so that their employees could use it.
