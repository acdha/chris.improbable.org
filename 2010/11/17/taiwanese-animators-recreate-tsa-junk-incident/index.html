Taiwanese Animators Recreate TSA ‘Junk’ Incident

If you’re still trying to understand how an airline passenger wound up under government investigation for refusing a TSA body scan and security grope at a San Diego airport, this new clip from the Taiwanese news-animation company NMA is your Zapruder film. It also covers the November 24 opt-out protest proposed by critics of the new invasive scanners, and the aggressive pat-downs waiting for those who decline an electronic strip search.
