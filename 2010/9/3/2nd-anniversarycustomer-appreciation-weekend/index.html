2nd Anniversary/Customer Appreciation Weekend

We opened Peregrine Espresso Labor Day weekend 2008.  This weekend, we will be celebrating our second anniversary with a couple of specials highlighting great relationships with a couple of our vendors.

We borrowed an extra grinder to feature a single-origin espresso from Counter Culture Coffee- Michicha Sundried from Ethiopia.  We also picked up some Trickling Springs Creamery Vanilla ice cream to make Michicha espresso floats.  Come by anytime Friday-Sunday (or until we run out) for a free Michicha espresso or from noon-close for a free Michicha espresso float.  Also, feel free to substitute the Michicha espresso for Espresso Apollo in any beverage.

Thanks for all the support over the past two years and we look forward to many more.

See you soon.
