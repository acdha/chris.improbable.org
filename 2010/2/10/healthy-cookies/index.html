Healthy Cookies

Healthy cookies might seem like a contradiction in terms but these carrot-raisin oatmeal cookies turned out quite well with, predictably, a few changes:

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 4 oz prunes
  • 2 oz dried cherries
  • 4 Tbs water
  • 3/83/4 cup honey
  • 1 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 2/3 cup raisins (Kristy doesn’t do raisins)
  • 1/2 cup almond meal
  • 22 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 cup oats
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp five-spice
  • 1/2 cup crushed pecans

Instructions

  1. Puree prunes and cherries with the water in a food processor until smooth.
  2. Place in a mixing bowl.
  3. Stir in honey, carrots.
  4. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough on a greased cookie sheet. Flatten slightly.
  6. Bake at 350275F degrees for 15 minutes or until set and just starting to brown. Remove to cooling racks.

Makes three dozen.
