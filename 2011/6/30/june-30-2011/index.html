BIG NEWS! (More info below)



 "For the past two years we've run a weekly web-only sketch show, and we've had an incredible time doing it. From meeting fans all across the country to making friends with exceptional artists, these past two years, and over 100 sketches, have been a whirlwind of activity - and one hell of an education. Now we're ready to take the next step.

See, we COULD have asked for your money two years ago when we started all of this, but we wouldn't have known what to do with it. Instead, we funded ourselves while we improved our craft. Our friends donated their time and effort every other weekend for two years. And we feel we made something special. But, after two years of hard work, we're beginning to realize there's only so much you can accomplish when you only have 8 hours to shoot something. We want to tell a story. We want to take the time and devote the necessary resources to make that story excellent. Oh, and we want to set it in space with dirty jokes.

That's right superpals, SMBC Theater is making the first of 3 (3!!) webseries - and we're making it a Space Opera. Want original, hilarious, high quality programming on the internet made by REAL nerds? Join us today. Every donation helps."
