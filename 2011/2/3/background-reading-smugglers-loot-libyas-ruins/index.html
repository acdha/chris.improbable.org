Background Reading: Smugglers loot Libya's ruins

http://www.thenational.ae/news/worldwide/africa/smugglers-loot-libyas-ruins?pageCount=0

This is only tangentially about Egypt but I think it's a valuable read at present because it indicates the scale of international antiquities smuggling.  It's also interesting because it indicates the rôle Egypt has perhaps been playing in the smuggling of Libyan antiquities. It's also worrying because it demonstrates that there may already be well-tested routes for smuggling antiquities out of Egypt so anything looted during the unrest might not long remain within Egypt.
