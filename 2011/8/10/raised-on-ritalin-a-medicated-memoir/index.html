Raised on Ritalin // Chapter 1

















Here is chapter 1 of what I'm planning on being a full-length book, where I will use my experiences with ADD and Ritalin to look at the disorder and its treatment from many angles.

I'm working on this during my spare time so it will probably be slow going with updates. I've already got the second chapter written and have started laying it out. I wanted to get this online right away so I could get some feedback, but once I've gotten a few chapters finished I'm planning to shop it around for publication as a book as well.

So far my rough outline looks like this:
-chapter 1: Introduction
-chapter 2: ADD/AHDH - definition and diagnostic criteria
-chapter 3: Ritalin and other meds
-chapter 4: DSM - examine the Diagnostics and Statistics Manual, its evolution, influence on modern psychiatry, and how disorders come to be defined
-chapter 5: ADD today - look at where the disorder is today, how it is defined, who's being diagnosed, the 'epidemic' of diagnoses over the last 10-15 years, medications (and their history, development, treatment value) and other possible treatments, and progress in determining the disorder's origins and what it exactly "is."
-chapter 6: living with ADD - how ADD informs who a person is who has it and pervades all aspects of their life: relationships, family, work, etc.
-chapter 6: the future

I welcome your comments, thoughts, suggestions, and stories of your own experiences with ADD. Also, PLEASE share and pass this around!
