Phil Bull on the Commercialization of Ubuntu

Phil Bull on the Commercialization of Ubuntu:

“Free software companies are already at a disadvantage compared to proprietary software businesses when it comes to making money, and insisting that all of their attempts to generate revenue fit into some warm, fuzzy picture of a benevolent cooperative for whom profit is incidental is unreasonable … If you want the Free desktop to be successful, to gain significant market share, to compete with some of the biggest, most ruthless companies in existence, you’re going to have to throw away some of your ideas about how this should work in an ideal world and start playing hard ball.”
