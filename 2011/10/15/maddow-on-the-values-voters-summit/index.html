Maddow on the Values Voters Summit

What Maddow and Brayton point out are interesting, but the interesting point I took away from watching these clips is that the Republicans have learned that if they hold the nation hostage they get what they want and they are going to do it over and over again. This is why you don't negotiate with terrorists.

Rachel Maddow asked an important question on her show after last weekend’s Values Voters Summit, where a steady stream of religious right loonies made outrageous and bigoted statements even about their fellow right wingers. Why does the media pay so little attention to it?

In 2008, a huge stink was made over some of the crazy statements made by Rev. Wright — and while some of them were indeed crazy, some of them were spot on — even after Obama repudiated those comments. And yet every single Republican candidate flocked to this gathering to appear with some of the craziest wingnuts imaginable, and the only person who made even a meek attempt to dispute anything that was said was Mitt Romney — and only after their bigotry was turned on him rather than on women, gay people or Muslims.
