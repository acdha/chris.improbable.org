The Money Meltdown

Lots of people enjoying and linking to this 11 min animation, narrative explaining the money crisis in this country.

I’ll give you two more good sources of info:

  • This American Life: The Giant Pool of Money — a 55 minute news show that first aired on NPR but is available on iTunes as an audio book for 99 cents. It explains why banks were so anxious to give out mortgages and how it all blew up in our faces.
  • Frontline: Inside the Meltdown — an hour special that goes into timeline detail on how the government has tried to responded to all the problems.

Personal thoughts: We are still in for a bumpy ride. The next 15 months or so are going to continue to be downhill. While Washington spending may soften the fall it still going to hurt pretty bad for everyone. The best you can do for yourself is keep your budget tight and learn to enjoy the simple things.

Source: http://blog.clickablebliss.com/2009/02/21/the-money-meltdown/
