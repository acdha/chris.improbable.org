OH CRAP.


Latest Michigan news:

“During its regular trustees meeting this morning, President Lou Anna Simon said MSU would be disproportionately damaged by the governor’s plan. That’s because a 3.1% cut to MSU’s state appropriations – dropping the university’s slice to $284 million — is compounded by a proposal to combine MSU’s extension services and experimental stations and cut the budgets of the two entities from $64 million combined to $32 million. (emphasis mine)

The extension service is a hallmark of the land-grant university. With offices covering the state, its mission is to disseminate research and university expertise that is important to Michiganders – from health and wellness to agriculture to food safety.“

What an amazingly stupid idea. Cutting back on Extension–when the state’s second largest industry, and the only viable one right now–is Agriculture…..

I have no words for how bad an idea that is.
