Ultimate zoom: the Infinite Photograph

Remember those mosaic photos that were hugely popular 10 years ago? A website called the Green Guide has taken that tired idea and created a mind-blowing interface to explore photos of Earth called the Infinite Photograph. You can just keep zooming — the picture seemingly goes on forever.

Even better, every individual photo in the collection has information about the location, the photographer, and all the tech specs — from exposure time to ISO. You can also contribute a picture of your own.

We love this site — we've been clicking on it all day — though we wish there was an option to see the pics in high-res, not to mention more pics in general (the mosaic kind of cheats by using several of the same photo in many spots). Still, the results are incredibly addictive.

Via The Green Guide
