Rod Parsley says ministry threatened by lack of funds | The Columbus Dispatch

World Harvest Church said in a statement this afternoon that the church has no plans toclose any ministries in 2010, including its schools, despite its Web site's assertion thatministries are "in jeopardy."

The Rev. Rod Parsley has issued a desperate plea for money, telling his flock that he isfacing a "demonically inspired financial attack" that is threatening his ministry.

…

When asked to comment yesterday, Parsley's World Harvest Church issued a statement sayingthe recession caused a decline in member giving in 2009, which has led to a fourth-quarter deficit of $3 million despite a 30 percent reduction in the budget.

This year, the church settled for $3.1 million with a family whose son was spanked at itsday-care center in 2006, to the point his buttocks and legs were covered with welts andabrasions.
